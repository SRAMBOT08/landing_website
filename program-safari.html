What is Design Safari?

Design Safari is an immersive, experiential learning journey where participants observe and analyze real-world design implementations in local contexts.

Through guided site visits and observation exercises, participants develop deeper insights into how design thinking principles are applied in practice across different environments and industries.

This hands-on experience complements the conference sessions by providing tangible examples of design in action.
