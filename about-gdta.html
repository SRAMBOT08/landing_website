About GDTA

Global Design Thinking Alliance - Indian Chapter

Our Organization

GDTA Indian Chapter

The Global Design Thinking Alliance (GDTA) Indian Chapter is a community of design thinking practitioners, educators, and enthusiasts committed to advancing human-centered innovation across India.

As part of the global GDTA network, we bring together professionals from diverse backgrounds to share knowledge, develop capabilities, and create impact through design thinking methodologies.

Our chapter focuses on building a vibrant ecosystem that supports innovation, collaboration, and meaningful change in organizations and society.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Mission

To empower individuals and organizations across India with design thinking capabilities, fostering innovation and human-centered problem-solving that creates positive impact.

Vision

To be the leading platform for design thinking practitioners in India, building a community that drives transformation through creativity, empathy, and collaborative innovation.
